About me

Engineer by craft, architect by experience, storyteller at heart.

I'm an AI Engineer and Solutions Architect based in the Netherlands, originally from South Africa. I focus on building applied AI — generative AI, computer vision, NLP, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and digital-twin experiences — and making sure they're production-ready, secure and scalable, not just notebook prototypes.

At Cyclomedia I work on the AI side of full-stack delivery, combining 20+ years of engineering and architecture with the Azure AI Engineer skillset. My cloud background (Azure Solutions Architect Expert, AWS, Terraform, TOGAF) is the foundation that keeps what I build deployable and reliable.

Beyond tech, I've performed stand-up comedy alongside Trevor Noah and released music as "Kraai" — experiences that taught me storytelling, presence and the value of connecting with an audience. I bring that same creativity to engineering.
